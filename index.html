Before you ask

I have never hosted an event before

Most first-time hosts have not. Registration, the leaderboard, medals, tees and the marketing assets are handled for you. You get the affiliate toolkit, two certifications for your coaches and a regional rep who has walked other owners through their first race day.

Is my space big enough

DEKA STRONG runs one lane in 1,200 sq ft as long as you have 40 ft of length. Two lanes need 1,800 sq ft. DEKA MILE needs 1,800 sq ft plus 80m of run space, indoor or out.

I am missing some equipment

Most gyms already own 8 of the 10 zones. Approved suppliers offer affiliate pricing, including 10% off list from Get RXd on Xebex sleds, and 12 month financing at 0% is available. Your rep can review your inventory before you commit.

Will this pull people away from my programming

It does the opposite. The DEKA Training System slots into your existing schedule as prep work, quarterly DEKA Challenges give members a reason to bring a friend, and event day puts non-members on your floor. Affiliates typically convert 1 to 3 new members per event.

What does it actually cost

$159 a month in the USA and $220 a month in Canada. The two certifications included in month one are valued at $745 on their own, and one hosted event at 60 participants returns roughly $2,160 to your gym.
